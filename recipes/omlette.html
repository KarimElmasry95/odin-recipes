Cheese Omelette Recipe

The perfect omelette

Eggs and cheese are the perfect combination for the perfect breakfast.

This recipe will demonstrate how you can make the perfect omelette with ease and simplicity.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • Sea salt and ground pepper (add as much as you prefer)
  • 1.5 ounces of brie cheese
  • 1 ounce of smoked cheddar cheese
  • 1 ounce of smoked havarti cheese

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the eggs, brie cheese, salt and pepper.
  2. Put a non-stick pan on medium heat.
  3. Put the mix in the pan and then sprinkle the cheddar and havarti on top of it.
  4. Give the edge of your omelette a nudge with a spatula, to see you can flip it.
  5. Gently fold it into a half circle shape.
  6. Wait 2 minutes then flip it over.
  7. Wait another 2 minutes then slide it on your plate.
