Lasagna Recipe

The perfect lasagna

This lasagna recipe is the perfect combination of ground meat, tomato sauce, and cheese. It’s the definition of decadence!

Lasagna isn't always the simplest dish to cook. But trust me, only one whiff of this masterpiece is enough to know that it’s worth it.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cups of ricotta, mozzarella, and parmigiano-reggiano
  • 1 pound of italian sausage or ground beef
  • 2 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 1 box of lasagna pasta
  • 2 Eggs

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Boil the pasta for 4 minutes.
  3. Mix sausage or ground beef and the marinara sauce. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix the ricotta, mozzarella, rarmigiano-reggiano, and eggs
  5. Grease a lasagna pan or a 9×13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the marinara sauce over the baking dish, about 3/4 cup.
  7. Layer 3 to 5 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Layer the cheese mixture of the noodles, followed by the meat sauce.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
